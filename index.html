HOW IT WORKS

We collect your unused or new donated books in exchange for a discount on future purchases as part of our book trade-in scheme.... These books are used to help children affected by unfortunate circumstances by providing them with schooling materials.
A portion of the money generated through new book sales is used to help provide for everyday school needs, such as meals, stationary and uniforms.

VISION & GOALS

We are working to help educate the children in our communities by aligning ourselves with the United Nations Sustainable Development goals. We believe a quality education is the foundation to improving lives and sustainable development.
